Bucket List: My Dream Travel Destinations

Places and events I want to experience when I'm rich

I have always loved to travel. As a child, my parents took me to many beautiful cities. I loved to explore the cities and their culture. We went to museums, historical sites, events and, my favorite, local restaurants. Exploring the local food culture was always exciting. My bucketlist of destinations are rich in all of these things.

Rome, Italy

I would like to explore the country of Italy as a whole, but my main focus would be Rome. The art, food and natural beauty of this city are well known. Italian food is one of my favorites. My family and I spend a lot of time on The Hill. I would loved to see all the beautiful architecture as well. Pictured below is St. Peter's Basilica

Morocco

Morocco is another country I would love to explore. There is a music festival called the Fes Festival of World Sacred Music that sounds amazing. There are also medieval sites to explore as well as beaches and mountains to explore. Jimi Hendrix and Bob Marley loved to hang out on the beaches of Essaouira. The city of Marrakesh (pictured below)has a lot history and markets to explore.

Maldives

I initially learned of the Maldives because my favorite power couple, Beyonce and Jay Z, honeymooned there. It is a sovereign state in South Asia. It is made up of 1,190 islands, so exploring the beaches and seafood would make for a great vacation. They also have complete all glass-undersea restaurants, which sound like an AMAZING meal and adventure. Pictured below is "Ithaa", the first of many underwater restaurants.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

I want to go to Rio during Carnival season. Carnival is a festival held prior to Lent. There are many Carnival celebrations across the world, but the one in Rio is the largest. The music, dance troupes and food look very exciting and I would love to experiece this at least once in my life.
